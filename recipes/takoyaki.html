Takoyaki

Here we have some delicious Takoyaki

Takoyaki, or Japanese octopus balls, are a beloved street food snack from Osaka. These crispy-on-the-outside, creamy-on-the-inside treats are made with a savory batter, pieces of tender octopus, and flavorful toppings like takoyaki sauce, Japanese mayo, and bonito flakes. Follow this step-by-step guide to making authentic takoyaki at home!

Ingredients

For the Batter:

  • 1 cup (240ml) dashi stock
  • 1 cup (120g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 tsp soy sauce
  • 1/4 tsp salt

For the Filling:

  • 4 oz (115g) cooked octopus, diced
  • 2 tbsp green onions, chopped
  • 2 tbsp tenkasu (tempura scraps)
  • 1 tbsp pickled ginger, minced

For Toppings:

  • Takoyaki sauce
  • Japanese mayonnaise
  • Bonito flakes (katsuobushi)
  • Aonori (seaweed flakes)

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Batter

  • In a bowl, whisk together dashi stock, flour, egg, soy sauce, and salt until smooth.

Step 2: Heat the Takoyaki Pan

  • Heat a takoyaki pan over medium heat and lightly grease each mold with oil.

Step 3: Cook the Takoyaki

  • Pour the batter into the molds, filling them about 3/4 full.
  • Add a piece of octopus, green onions, tenkasu, and pickled ginger into each mold.
  • Once the edges start to cook, use skewers to rotate the balls, turning them as they brown.
  • Continue turning until the takoyaki are golden brown and fully cooked.

Step 4: Add Toppings

  • Drizzle takoyaki sauce and Japanese mayonnaise over the cooked takoyaki.
  • Sprinkle with bonito flakes and aonori for extra flavor.

Step 5: Serve & Enjoy!

  • Serve immediately while hot and enjoy these delicious homemade takoyaki!
